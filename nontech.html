Non-Technical Events

Unleash your creativity and have fun in exciting challenges!

Stellar Frames

A cinema-themed event where participants engage in games related to songs, music, and movies. It involves identifying movie scenes, guessing soundtracks using audios and images, making it a fun challenge for movie lovers..

Rounds: 3

Participants: Individual or Team (Max 2)

Time: 02:15 PM - 03:15 PM

Interstellar Echo

A telepathy-based game where participants help their partners guess words or phrases without speaking directly. This can involve being blindfolded, using headphones, or other creative methods. The event tests non-verbal communication and synchronization between teammates.

Rounds: 2

Participants: Team (Max 2)

Time: 02:15 PM - 03:15 PM

Zero Gravity Games

A collection of fun-filled mini-games using balloons, paper cuts, and other props. Participants engage in multiple activities that require quick thinking, coordination, and teamwork, making it an exciting and energetic event.

Rounds: 2

Participants: Team (Max 2)

Time: 02:15 PM - 03:15 PM

Galactic Detective

A mystery-solving event where participants play detective roles, solving crime scenes, answering riddles, and searching for hidden clues. It challenges analytical thinking, observation skills, and logical reasoning in a thrilling and immersive experience.

Rounds: Multiple

Participants: Team (Max 2)

Time: 02:15 PM - 03:15 PM

Nebula Recall

A memory-based event featuring multiple rounds that test participants' recall abilities. It includes games focused on remembering sequences, spotting changes, and identifying patterns, making it a perfect challenge for those with sharp memory skills.

Rounds: 1

Participants: Individual

Time: 02:15 PM - 03:15 PM

About ELECXY-2K25

ELECXY-2K25 is a National level symposium conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). The event is themed around the vast Galaxy, symbolizing infinite possibilities in electronics and innovation.

Highlights of Our College & Department

Our College:

  • Autonomous Status: Granted by the University Grants Commission for a decade, from 2019 to 2029.
  • Research Recognition: Recognized as a Scientific & Industrial Research Organization by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR).
  • Completed projects funded by government agencies like DST, DBT, and CSIR, totaling ₹3.6 Crore.

Department of ECE:

  • Accredited by NBA and recognized with NAAC ‘A’ Grade.
  • Offers autonomous status with advanced laboratories and industry collaborations.
  • Focuses on research, innovation, and practical learning.
  • Strong placement record with mentorship for higher studies and competitive exams.

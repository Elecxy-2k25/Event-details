About ELECXY-2K25

ELECXY-2K25 is a National level symposium conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). The event is themed around the vast Galaxy, symbolizing infinite possibilities in electronics and innovation.

Highlights of Our College & Department

Our College:Our College was established in the year 1998. It is promoted and supported by Virudhunagar Hindu Nadars' Devasthanam, various Hindu Nadars' Mahamai Tharappus in Virudhunagar and other places and educational institutions of Virudhunagar. The management of the institution consists of the elected members of various Mahamai Tharappus and ex-officio members of various educational institutions of Virudhunagar. The office bearers, the president, the vice-president, the secretary, the joint secretary and the treasurer - are elected by the managing board members. Our College is ideally located on the home land of Karmaveerar "Bharat Ratna" K. Kamaraj and our institution is one of the Virudhunagar's most recognisable landmark. Our beloved Patron Thiru.A.S.K.A.M. Nagarajan magnanimously donated 50 Lakhs and Our Founder Secretary Er.S.P.G.C. Srimurugan has donated 25 acres of Land for the progress of our college. Our Institution is growing with sustained progress due to the generous contributions from Virudhunagar Devasthanam, Various Mahamai Tharappus and Educational Institutions. Presently, Our College spans to 47.92 acres of land.

Department of ECE:The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at our institution stands as a hub of academic excellence, research, and innovation. With a strong foundation in technical education, the department ensures that students gain in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience to solve real-time challenges in the field of electronics and communication. Accredited by NBA and recognized with NAAC ‘A’ Grade, the department has also achieved autonomous status, reflecting its commitment to delivering high-quality education. Emphasizing research and development, it hosts advanced laboratories and collaborates with industries to drive innovation and practical learning. The faculty members, with expertise in diverse domains, play a crucial role in mentoring students for placements, higher studies, and competitive examinations. Moreover, the department fosters a culture of creativity and professional growth through technical clubs, industry tie-ups, and skill-enhancement programs, ensuring that graduates are well-equipped to meet the evolving demands of the industry.

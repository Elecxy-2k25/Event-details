About ELECXY-2K25

ELECXY-2K25 is a National level symposium conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). The event is themed around the vast Galaxy, symbolizing infinite possibilities in electronics and innovation.

Highlights of Our College & Department

Our College: Kamaraj College of Engineering and Technology is an autonomous institution known for academic excellence, research, and industry collaborations.

Department of ECE: Recognized for innovation and technological advancements, our department actively engages in research and organizes national-level events like ELECXY.

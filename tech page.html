Technical Events

Explore the galaxy of technical challenges and innovations

Paper Way Galaxy - Paper Presentation

A formal paper presentation event where participants can choose any technical topic and present their research or findings. This allows them to showcase their knowledge, improve their presentation skills, and receive feedback from experts. It encourages innovation and deep understanding of the subject matter.

Rounds: 2

Participants: Individual or Team (Max 2)

Time: 11:30 AM - 1:15 PM

Venue: Canteen block conference hall

Prize: Cash Prize

Star Tech Expo - Project Exhibition

A technical project exhibition where participants display and demonstrate their working models or non-working prototypes. They explain their projects to panel members, highlighting the technical aspects, innovations, and real-world applications. This event fosters creativity and problem-solving skills.

Rounds: 1

Participants: Team (Max 2)

Time: 11:30 AM - 01:15 PM

Venue: Ece Lab 1

Prize: Cash Prize

Signal Sync

A word-guessing game where participants work in pairs to communicate technical terms creatively. One person gives hints using related words without saying the actual word, while their partner guesses it. This event tests communication skills, vocabulary, and quick thinking.

Rounds: 2

Participants: Team (Max 2)

Time: 11:30 AM - 01:15 PM

Venue: D10 Hall

Prize:Exciting prizes

GalaxIQ - Interview Based Technical Quiz

A mock interview-based event where participants go through multiple interview rounds simulating real-world hiring processes. They experience technical and HR questions, group discussions, and problem-solving challenges. The best performer is selected based on their confidence, knowledge, and adaptability.

Rounds: 3

Participants: Individual

Time: 11:30 AM - 01:15 PM

Venue:D9 hall

Prize: Cash Prize

Cosmic Canvas - Creative Poster Presentation

A creative poster presentation event where participants design and showcase visually appealing posters on technical topics. This platform allows them to present ideas innovatively through graphics, charts, and concise content. It encourages creativity, effective communication, and knowledge sharing. Participants can engage with viewers, discuss their concepts, and receive valuable feedback.

Rounds: 1

Participants: Individual or Team (Max 2)

Time: 11:30 AM - 01:15 PM

Venue: Canteen block conference hall

Prize: Cash Prize
